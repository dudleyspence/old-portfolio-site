Peace Of Mind (In Development)

Frontend: JavaScript, React.js, next.js, tailwind

Backend: Node.js, express.js, MongoDB

Peace of Mind bridges the communication gap between family members of geriatric patients and their carers. The platform provides a centralized space where guardians can outline daily tasks—agreed upon with the carer—such as medication schedules, hygiene routines, and meal plans, as well as set day-specific tasks like medical appointments.

Carers can log task completions and leave notes on enrichment activities, such as walks or puzzles. At the end of each visit, carers provide a wellness score. This well-being data offers peace of mind by allowing families to easily track the care and comfort of their loved ones.
